**Effective Date:** Follows the page content update date.

## 1. Introduction

Xone Chain is a leading behavior-value-incentivized public blockchain (BVI). In Xone Chain, every interaction creates value, and every contribution deserves encouragement. Xone Chain is a decentralized blockchain platform that utilizes the native XOC token for transaction fees, decentralized governance, and rewards within the Xone ecosystem. The donation program aims to build consensus around Xone.

## 2. Eligibility

To participate in the donation, participants must:

- Be at least 18 years old;
- Not be residents or citizens of countries where participation in donations is prohibited or restricted (e.g., the United States, China);
- Understand and accept the risks associated with cryptocurrency and blockchain-related projects.

## 3. XOC Coin

XOC Coin is the native cryptocurrency of Xone Chain. It will be used in the Xone ecosystem for transaction fees, staking, governance voting, and rewards. XOC Coin does not represent any ownership, control, or security interest in Xone Chain.

## 4. General Donation Program

- During the donation period, payments can be made using supported cryptocurrencies, specifically USDH;
- Each participant may donate in 10 increments, each valued at 100.00 USDH, and receive 666 XOC in return;
- Total donation allocation: 45,000,000.00 XOC (representing 4.5% of the total allocation).

## 5. Knight Donation Program

Each knight may donate one increment valued at 10,000.00 USDH and receive 9 exclusive benefits:

1. Access to exclusive Xone platform NFTs;
2. Eligibility to recommend high-quality projects;
3. Honorary certificate issued by the Foundation;
4. Subsidies for community member referrals;
5. Node mining income as a member of the Xone organization;
6. Priority in investment opportunities;
7. Eligibility to participate in global events hosted by Xone Chain;
8. Priority access to airdrop benefits within the Xone ecosystem;
9. On-site access to the Foundation's headquarters, providing insight into the Xone ecosystem as well as blockchain and Web3 training.

## 6. Tokenomics

Total Supply: 10 billion XOC

Distribution Plan:

- BVI Nodes: 50% (5 billion) – Distributed based on contributions within the BVI system;
- Staking Rewards: 10% (1 billion tokens) – Released based on staking weights and governance;
- Donation Plan: 10% (1 billion tokens) – 5% released upon launch, with the remainder released over two years; excess tokens may be burned;
- Team Allocation: 10% (1 billion tokens) – Released over three years;
- Ecosystem Fund: 10% (1 billion tokens) – Used for exchange listings and ecosystem governance;
- DAO and Airdrop: 10% (1 billion tokens) – Released through DAO and airdrop activities.
