This guide is designed to help you successfully complete the USDH swap process by providing detailed steps, from accessing the official website to finalizing the transaction. Through this swap, you will be able to acquire the necessary USDH and use it within the Xone Chain ecosystem. Please read the following steps carefully to ensure a smooth operation.

## Process

<Steps>
### Step 1: Access the Huione Swap Official Website
 

1. Open your browser and visit or enter the URL: https://usdh.huionedefi.com

2. In the homepage navigation bar, locate and click on [**Swap**] to enter the swap page.
 
### Step 2: View USDH Information
 

1. On the swap page, you can view detailed information about USDH, including the exchange rate and swap rules. Make sure you understand the relevant terms and conditions to make an informed decision.

2. USDH currently supports swaps on three mainnets: Ethereum, Tron, and BnbChain. Solana is not yet available. The corresponding contract information is as follows:

### Step 3: Connect Your Wallet
 
Locate the [**Connect Wallet**] button on the page and click it. Choose your preferred digital wallet (such as MetaMask, TronLink，OKX Wallet). Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the wallet connection, and the system will automatically retrieve your wallet address and the USDT balance in your wallet.

### Step 4: Enter the Swap Amount
 
After confirming the USDT/USDH swap details, enter the amount you wish to swap in the **From** field.

<Callout type="info" emoji="ℹ️">
  **Note:**\
  1.The USDT to USDH swap ratio is 1:1.\
  2.Please ensure that your connected wallet has sufficient USDT balance.\
  3.Make sure the swap amount is within the allowable range and complies with Huione DeFi's relevant regulations.
</Callout>


### Step 5: Confirm Information
 
1. Carefully verify the swap information, including the swap amount and receiving address.

2. If all the information is correct, click the [**Swap**] button.

### Step 6: Transaction Authorization
 
1. After clicking [**Swap**], the system will prompt you to authorize the transaction.

2. Confirm the authorization request to ensure that you grant Huione DeFi permission to process the transaction.

### Step 7: Confirm the Transaction
 
Check the transaction details in your wallet to ensure the information is accurate, then click [**Confirm**] to complete the swap.

### Step 8: Transaction Completed
 
After the transaction is completed, you will receive a confirmation message indicating that your USDH swap was successful.
