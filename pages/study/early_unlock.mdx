# Waiver Operation Guide

<Video src="video/Waiver_Operation.mp4"/>

In the [Xone](https://xone.org/) donation release system, **Waiver** refers to the donor‚Äôs ability to **voluntarily give up the unreleased portion** of their allocated tokens. Upon waiver, the corresponding unreleased amount is **immediately redirected to the Foundation**, and the donor receives a **1:1 minted RWA Options Token (ROT)** as compensation.

This allows donors to shift from a **locked principal** model to an **immediate liquidity + dividend participation** model, based on RWA performance.

## üß† FAQ

<details>

<summary>Q1: Can I cancel my waiver?</summary>

No. Once you submit a waiver transaction, it is irreversible and final.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Q2: Is ROT guaranteed to hold or increase value?</summary>

No. ROT value depends on RWA dividend performance and market trading. It has **no fixed redemption value**.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Q3: What happens to the tokens I waived?</summary>

They are permanently redirected to the **[Xone](https://xone.org/) Foundation wallet** and will no longer belong to the donor.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Q4: Are there tax implications?</summary>

Possibly. ROT issuance and dividend income may be treated differently by regulators in different jurisdictions. Consult your local tax advisor.

</details>
