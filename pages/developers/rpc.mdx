## How to Connect to Xone

### **For MetaMask**
1. Open MetaMask and click on your profile icon.
2. Navigate to **Settings** > **Networks** > **Add Network**.
3. Enter the following details:

<ImageViewer src="/images/metaMask_add_network.png" alt="MetaMask Add Network" />

4. Save the network and start interacting with the [Xone](https://xone.org/) TestNet.
