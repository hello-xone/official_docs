## How to Connect to Xone

### **For MetaMask**
1. Open MetaMask and click on your profile icon.
2. Navigate to **Settings** > **Networks** > **Add Network**.
3. Enter the following details:

![add testnet to metamask](public/images/metamask_testrpc.png)

4. Save the network and start interacting with the Xone TestNet.

#### **For Developers**
Use the **New RPC URL** (https://rpc.xonetest.plus/) in your applicationâ€™s configurations to connect to the Xone Testnet. The RPC endpoint supports all standard Ethereum JSON-RPC methods.

### **Block Explorer**
The block explorer for Xone Testnet is available at [https://testnet.xscscan.com/](https://testnet.xscscan.com/).  
You can use it to:
- View transactions.
- Check account balances.
- Explore smart contracts.
- Monitor network activity.
