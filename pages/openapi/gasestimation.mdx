## Gas estimation

Estimates gas fees for transactions and provides historical and current gas price data.

[eth_feeHistory](/openapi/gasestimation/eth_feeHistory) : Retrieves historical gas fee data over a range of blocks. \
[eth_estimateGas](/openapi/gasestimation/eth_estimateGas) : Estimates the gas required for executing a transaction. \
[eth_gasPrice](/openapi/gasestimation/eth_gasPrice) : Returns the current gas price on the network. \
[eth_createAccessList](/openapi/gasestimation/eth_createAccessList) : Generates an access list to optimize gas costs. \
[eth_maxPriorityFeePerGas](/openapi/gasestimation/eth_maxPriorityFeePerGas) : Retrieves the maximum priority fee per gas unit.

These methods help optimize transaction costs on Xone.
