To enhance accessibility, improve user experience, and optimize services, [Xone](https://xone.org/) is updating its domains. This change aligns with our commitment to delivering seamless and efficient interactions across the [Xone](https://xone.org/) ecosystem.

## 🕒 Effective Date

December 24, 3:00 PM (UTC+8)

## 🌐 New Domains:

- **Website:** From `https://xone.plus` to `https://xone.org`
- **Faucet:** From `https://faucet.xone.plus` to `https://faucet.xone.org`
- **Docs:** From `https://docs.xone.plus` to `https://docs.xone.org`

Both `www` and root domains will be supported.

### 1️⃣ Transition Period

The current xone.plus domains will remain accessible until Friday, December 29, 2024. After this date, only xone.org will be operational.

Please take a moment to update your bookmarks, tools, and any references to ensure uninterrupted access to our services.

We appreciate your understanding and support during this transition. These updates are part of our ongoing efforts to enhance your experience within the [XONE](https://xone.org/) ecosystem.
